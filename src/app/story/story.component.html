Groom & Bride

We started talking after being introduced by a mutual contact shortly after I graduated. After months of talking and getting to know each other over the phone, Josh flew across the sea to finally meet me in person. From the moment we first met he stole my heart. Next thing you know he proposes on Valentines day and the rest is history.

Josh Barbee
